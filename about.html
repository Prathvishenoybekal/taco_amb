About TTS

  • Torchy's Tacos is a fast-casual restaurant chain that serves "damn good tacos" in Texas and other states.

    • The first Torchy's Tacos opened in Houston, Texas in , and the company has since expanded to over 100 locations in Texas, Colorado, Oklahoma, Arkansas, Louisiana, and North Carolina.
  • Torchy's Tacos is known for its creative and flavorful tacos, which are made with fresh ingredients.

    •     The menu features a variety of tacos, including the Trailer Park Trash, which is topped with fried chicken, green chiles, cheese, and pico de gallo; the Green Chile Queso Taco, which is filled with melted cheese, green chiles, and pico de gallo; and the Brushfire Chicken Taco, which is made with grilled chicken, roasted corn, avocado, and chipotle mayo.
  • In addition to tacos, Torchy's Tacos also serves a variety of other items, including quesadillas, nachos, and breakfast tacos. The restaurant also has a full bar, which serves beer, wine, and cocktails.
