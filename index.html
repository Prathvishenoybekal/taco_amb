About TTS

  • Torchy's Taco Shop has been a staple in the community for over 50 years. It was founded in 1972 by Victor Hernandez, a Mexican immigrant who came to the United States with dreams of opening his own restaurant. Victor started out small, selling tacos out of a food truck in the parking lot of a local grocery store. But his tacos were so delicious that word quickly spread, and soon he was able to open a brick-and-mortar shop.

  •  Torchy's Taco Shop has always been known for its authentic Mexican cuisine. The tacos are made with fresh, high-quality ingredients, and the recipes are all family secrets. Torchy's also has a wide variety of other Mexican dishes, like burritos, enchiladas, and quesadillas.

  •   Over the years, Torchy's Taco Shop has become a beloved institution in the community. It's a place where people come to celebrate special occasions, to catch up with friends, or simply to enjoy a delicious meal. Victor's is a reminder of the rich Mexican culture that is a part of the fabric of this city.

  •  One of the things that makes Torchy's Taco Shop so special is its family atmosphere. Victor's[The ownwer] children and grandchildren all work in the restaurant, and they all take great pride in continuing the tradition that their father started. Victor's Taco Shop is a place where people feel welcome and at home, and it's a place where memories are made.

Taco Trivia

When did taco first appear in The United States of America?

The first mention of a taco in the United States was in a newspaper in 1905 .

This was around the time that Mexican migrant workers began to come to the Southwestern United States in search of work. They brought the taco with them, and it quickly became popular among other Mexican immigrants.
